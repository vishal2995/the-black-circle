Qualification Requirements

Exclusive Access. Exceptional Service.

At The Black Circle, we serve a select group of individuals, family offices, and institutions who meet our internal thresholds and the regulatory standards of the Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM) under FSRA supervision.

We ensure every client is classified and onboarded in accordance with FSRA rules, including full AML/KYC procedures and CRS/FATCA compliance.

For Individual Clients
  • Minimum 500k USD in digital assets or equivalent in cash and an initial deposit.
  • Demonstrated experience with institutional-grade financial services.
  • Eligible for classification as a Professional Client under FSRA standards.
  • Willingness to complete AML/KYC, Source of Funds, and tax disclosure process.

What is an FSRA Professional Client?

A client with at least USD 1 million in net assets (excluding primary residence) and sufficient financial knowledge or experience. Classification affects the level of regulatory protections and required disclosures.
